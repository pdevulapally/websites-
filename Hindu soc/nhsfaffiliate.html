About NHSF

The National Hindu Students' Forum (NHSF) is the largest Hindu student body in the UK, dedicated to promoting Hindu Dharma and supporting students in their spiritual and academic journeys. Established in 1991, NHSF aims to create a platform for Hindu students to connect, share, and grow through various cultural, educational, and community activities.

NHSF works to preserve, protect, and promote Hindu traditions, values, and principles among students. Through a range of events, workshops, and initiatives, NHSF fosters a sense of belonging and provides opportunities for personal and spiritual growth. By affiliating with various university Hindu societies, NHSF creates a strong network of students who can collaborate, share resources, and celebrate their heritage together.
